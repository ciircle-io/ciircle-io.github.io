Why do you call it a vow?

It is just a solemn promise. You make it to yourself, so you can make of it what you will.

There are some unfortunate associations with vows, but other words just don't seem to capture the gravity of this sort of personal promise. I think the imprtant thing is to remember that it is personal -- you only make it to yourself.
